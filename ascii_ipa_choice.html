ASCII IPA files
 

There are three ASCII IPA guides at this Web site. They are:

"Nutshell" a quick look at the symbols used in ASCII IPA
"Combined" a more comprehensive guide, with both American and British examples, and a few examples of German and French pronunciations
"American Only" a version of "Combined" with American examples only, for those not interested in the sounds of British English or other European languages

These additional pages contain parallel texts in IPA and "ordinary English":

  • Interlinear ASCII IPA Examples
  • Pronunciation of newsgroup contributors' names

See also:

  • Evan Kirshenbaum's ASCII IPA Specification in PDF format, or
  • Evan Kirshenbaum's ASCII IPA Specification in HTML format, and
  • The University of Iowa's Phonetics Flash Animation Project
