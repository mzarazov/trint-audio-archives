London Boink

14 July 2002
 

The minuets of the 14 July 2002 London Boink were posted to the newsgroup by "Jamie Vann". Click here to see the posting and the rest of the thread in the Google archives. Mike Connally took some photos. Click on the thumbnails below to see the bigger pictures.


Mike Page reports Osama's threat to become accountant.

And scoffs at him for missing the window of opportunity.

Jitze Couperus and Graeme Thomas join in scoffing.

Jitze describes homologation, requiring 500 examples.

Laura Spira declines homologation; Els bemused.

"Jamie Vann" fetches a brace of Prides & other minueted things.

"Jamie" hears Jitze explain precision bombing at 87 knots.

Graeme deconstructs the senselessness of E.

Jitze didn't mean to pee on elephant. Mistook for rubber tree.

"Jamie" hears Jitze tell of lagridge performed on eucalyptus.

Totally-Official minuets being taken.

Laura wonders at Truly's 'any more'.

Graeme delivers Totally-Official citation.

"Jamie" receives citation.

Graeme congratulates ace-boinker "Jamie".

"Jamie" displays neck orders.

But is soon captivated by another of Jitze's tales.
See also Tony Cooper's Totally Unofficial Portrait.
