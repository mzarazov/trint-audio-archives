Commonest words

by Mark Israel
  
     [This is a fast-access FAQ excerpt.]

According to the Guinness Book of World Records, the commonest
word in written English is "the," followed by: of, and, to, a, in,
that, is, I, it, for, as.  The commonest word in the King James
Version of the Bible is also "the".  The commonest word in spoken
English is "I."

   Frequency Analysis of English Vocabulary and Grammar: Based on
the LOB Corpus by Stig Johansson and Knut Hofland (OUP, 1989, ISBN
0-19-8242212-2) gives the top eighteen words and their frequencies
as:

      1.  the       68315
      2.  of        35716
      3.  and       27856
      4.  to        26760
      5.  a         22744
      6.  in        21108
      7.  that      11188
      8.  is        10978
      9.  was       10499
     10.  it        10010
     11.  for        9299
     12.  he         8776
     13.  as         7337
     14.  with       7197
     15.  be         7186
     16.  on         7027
     17.  I          6696
     18.  his        6266
