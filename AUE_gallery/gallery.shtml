You can have your picture in the AUE Photo Gallery. All you need do is send a GIF or JPG file (or the location of one, on the Web) to me, the webmaster, and I will install it in the gallery. I can crop and scale your photo to make a thumbnail for use above, or you can send me your own thumbnail image if you like. Please state the exact name you'd like me to use. If you'd like a short paragraph under your photo, perhaps with your e-mail address, or a link to your home page, that's fine as well. Two photos, one of you as a baby? No problem. I'm here to serve.

At the Totally Official Web site, there was a photo album where the names associated with the pictures are carefully withheld from the public. That album no longer exists, but on this site there is archived a modified copy of the Totally Official album.

See also the boink reports.
